Considering Bringing Your Pet?

A very common question: can I bring a dog? It IS a Morrison party; you would think this is a no brainer. However, due to the amount of people/food, bringing your 4-legged friend might get a little furry, and because of that, we are discouraging it. If you do bring your pup, please make sure to keep an eye on them.
